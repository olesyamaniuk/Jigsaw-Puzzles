Difficulty Levels

You Choose the Challenge

Whether you're looking for a quick 12-piece coffee-break puzzle or an evening-long 625-piece marathon, Jigsaw Puzzles Titan puts the power in your hands. Select from 11 unique levels and control every aspect of your puzzle experience—from grid layout to whether pieces are rotated.

Personalization & Custom Puzzles

Create Your Own Puzzle Adventures

Your memories, your way. Upload your favorite family photos, vacation shots, or artwork and turn them into high-quality puzzles. All customization options—difficulty, piece shape, rotation—are available for your custom puzzles too.
These puzzles are especially popular for birthdays, memory games, or simply reliving a meaningful moment in a new, interactive form.

Offline Play

Peace of Mind, No Connection Needed

We’ve made sure your experience isn’t tied to Wi-Fi or mobile data. Once downloaded, all puzzles are fully available offline. Whether you're traveling, at a cabin, or simply prefer playing unplugged, Jigsaw Puzzles Titan has you covered.
And yes—your progress is saved automatically. You can switch between puzzles, pause, or take a break without losing a single piece.

Achievements & Goals

A Rewarding Path for Every Player

Progress feels more satisfying when it's recognized. Jigsaw Puzzles Titan includes achievement tracking and reward systems that encourage exploration, consistency, and mastery.
Complete puzzles in record time, solve without using hints, or finish a set of daily puzzles in a row to unlock unique in-game badges and milestones. Whether you’re competitive or casual, achievements provide a sense of direction and reward.

Daily Free Puzzle

A Fresh Start, Every Day

Begin each day with a new challenge—our daily free puzzle is hand-picked to offer variety and fun. These puzzles are time-limited exclusives, available only for a short window, so be sure to check in often.
They’re a perfect way to test your skills, discover new themes, and earn streak-based rewards. And of course, they’re always free to play.
